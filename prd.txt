1. Vision
Build a premium OTT streaming platform that delivers a theatrical experience on the web. Designed for high-end content distribution with a focus on speed, adaptive quality, and modern aesthetics.

2. Problem Statement
Existing video platforms are often generic and lack the "Cinema-First" design required by high-end creators and niche organizations. StreamHive solves this by providing a professional, self-hosted streaming ecosystem.

3. Key Goals
- Deliver zero-buffer adaptive bitrate streaming via HLS.
- Provide a cinematic, high-standard UI/UX.
- Enable robust content management for admins.
- Ensure cross-device compatibility (Web, mobile-responsive).

4. Target Users
- Viewers: Seeking a distraction-free, high-quality streaming experience.
- Admins/Creators: Managing libraries, uploading content, and monitoring views.

5. Technical Requirements
- Frontend: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
- Backend: Node.js, Express, TypeScript.
- Database: MongoDB.
- Video Engine: FFmpeg (HLS Transcoding).
- Authentication: JSON Web Tokens (JWT).

6. Core Features
- Secure Authentication: Sign up, Login, and Password Reset flows.
- Cinema Discovery: Hero features, genre grids, and language-based rows.
- Advanced Player: HLS playback with adaptive quality switching.
- Creator Studio: Admin dashboard for video publishing and metadata management.
- Watch Progress: Track history and resume videos seamlessly.

7. UX Principles
- Content-First Layout.
- Cinema-Gold Aesthetic.
- Fluid Micro-interactions.
- Professional Navigation Standards.
